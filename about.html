About Us

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to innovation and continuous improvement in the surveying and construction sectors.

Who We Are

As pioneers in adopting DGPS technology, we pride ourselves on our team's dedication and expertise. Our motto, "Service before Self," emphasizes our commitment to prioritizing client needs.

Over the years, we’ve evolved into a comprehensive solution provider, offering a range of services and building a reputation for excellence through hard work and fair business practices. We’ve successfully completed projects for numerous national organizations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver innovative, high-quality technology solutions that help businesses achieve operational excellence. We aim to provide exceptional value to our clients by continuously evolving and adapting to the ever-changing technological landscape.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in IT solutions, empowering businesses through technology that drives growth and success. We aspire to create long-term value by building lasting relationships with our clients and fostering a culture of innovation and creativity within our team.

Our History

Founded in 1994, In line with our growth, we are excited to announce the launch of our new IT Division, focusing on software development and services. This division will create tailored software solutions that enhance efficiency, accuracy, and scalability, integrating our surveying knowledge with cutting-edge technology. By integrating software development with our expertise in surveying, we aim to deliver even more comprehensive, efficient, and scalable solutions.

Our journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence, a drive to stay ahead of industry trends, and an unwavering commitment to our clients’ success. Today, we are proud to have a diverse team of experts working together to deliver exceptional results.
